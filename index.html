Best 3 series according to Oliver

My top three series of all time and why...

The Walking Dead

A post-apocalyptic nightmare with zombies. Rick Grimes, the protagonist, wakes from a coma to find the world has been consumed by a mysterious zombie outbreak. In search of his missing family, Rick must make decisions that break the moral code of the old world he once knew in order to survive.

Snowpiercer

Scientists freeze the planet due to rising temperatures from climate change, causing the earth to become an uninhabitable ball of ice. Billionaire engineer Mr. Wilford builds a train designed to become an ark capable of saving humanity. But once on board, the passengers are ruled over by a strict class system, enabling brutal fascist dictators to rise to power. A dystopian nightmare with revolutionary ideals at its core.

Lost

A passenger plane flying from Sydney to Los Angeles crash-lands in the Pacific Ocean on a mysterious island. A group of survivors, all with demons in their past, must now band together in order to survive. The longer they remain, the more mysterious the island becomes, creating a rich, mind-bending narrative that questions the very nature of reality.

What I look for in a series or story...

  • Dystopian themes
  • A rich narrative that questions morality and social norms
  • Existential depth
  • A journey
